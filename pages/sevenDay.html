7 Day Programme

Select Day 1 to start (or restart) the 7 day programme. Once you have started the programme you don't have to stick to the suggested exercises, you can do any exercise you choose. Try and commit to doing 5 minutes a day on one of the exercises.

Once you have started the programme, you will see a 'done' button on each exercise page. When you have completed the exercise, select the 'done' button so that BeCalm can help you see your progress through the programme.
