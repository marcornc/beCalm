Measuring Progress

In order to help measure your own progress, please answer the following 5 questions. You will be asked the same feedback questions again in 7 days time so you can track your own progress.

When you click submit, your feedback scores will be anonymously shared with the app development team in order to help us understand how useful the app is and any improvements that can be made.
